Population Statistics

According to the latest estimates, the population of Ethiopia is approximately 117 million people.

Year Population

The population of Ethiopia is projected to reach 200 million by 2050.

Ethnic Groups

Ethiopia is a diverse country with more than 80 ethnic groups.

  • Oromo
  • Amhara
  • Somali
  • Tigray
  • Sidama

The Oromo people are the largest ethnic group in Ethiopia, comprising approximately 34% of the population.

Migration Patterns

Ethiopia has a long history of migration, both within the country and to other parts of the world.

  1. Internal Migration

    2. Many Ethiopians migrate within the country in search of better economic opportunities or to escape conflict or environmental disasters.

  2. International Migration

    3. Many Ethiopians also migrate to other countries, particularly to the Middle East and Europe, in search of better economic opportunities.
